Moving Sketch Entities in Freehand Sketches

In freehand sketches, you can translate and rotate freehand curves.

  1. From the Freehand section of the action bar, click Drag .

  2. Select one or more freehand curves to move or rotate.

    The Robot appears on the first curve you select.

  3. Drag the handles of the Robot reposition the curve.

  4. Drag the Robot by an external arc to rotate the curve.

  5. Hover the pointer over the Robot origin to show a blue sphere. Select the sphere to move the curve.
