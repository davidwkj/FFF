Creating Circular Patterns of Components

Context:

To create a circular pattern of components:

  1. From the Assembly section of the action bar, click Circular Component Pattern .

  2. For Components to Pattern , select one or more components to pattern.

  3. For Axis of Rotation , select an axis, cylindrical face, linear edge, or line.

  4. For Spacing Angle , enter the angle between instances.

  5. For Number of Instances , enter the number of instances, including the seed component, of the component.

  6. In the dialog box, click More Options to specify additional options.
