Mirroring a Feature

You can mirror one or more features, bodies, or reference geometry.

  1. From the section of the action bar, click Mirror .

  2. Optional. Click Body Selection to mirror bodies.

  3. For Mirror Plane Selection , perform one of the following actions:

    • Click in Mirror Plane Selection and select a planar face or reference plane in the work area.

    • Click Reference Plane to create a mirror plane.

  4. Select the features to mirror.
