Creating Fillets

You can use fillets to create a rounded internal or external face on a product.

  1. From the section of the action bar, click Fillet .

  2. In the work area, select edges or faces to fillet.

  3. To specify the fillet size, do one of the following:

    • Drag the handle.

    • Enter a value.

  4. Specify options in the Fillet dialog box and click .
