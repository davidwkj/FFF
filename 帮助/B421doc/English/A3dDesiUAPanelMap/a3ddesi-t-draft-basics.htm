Creating a Draft Feature

You can use the draft feature to taper the selected faces of a product by a specified angle.

  1. From the section of the action bar, click Draft .

  2. Select the following:

    • The plane or planar faces to define the draft.
    • The options to define the draft parameters.

  3. Specify the draft angle by dragging the handle or entering a value in the callout in the work area.
