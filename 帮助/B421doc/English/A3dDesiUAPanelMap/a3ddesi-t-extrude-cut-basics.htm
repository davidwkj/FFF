Creating an Extrude or Extrude Cut

You can linearly project a sketch to add or remove material from the model.

  1. From the section of the action bar, click Extrude or Extrude Cut .

    Tip: In the dialog box, to toggle between Extrude and Extrude Cut, click Cut . The Cut option is available under the Add option.

  2. Select a sketch or sketch region to extrude.

  3. To specify the height, drag the handle or enter a value in the dialog box.

  4. In the dialog box, select the extrude type and specify the end conditions.

  5. For Extrude Cut , click Reverse Side to Cut to remove material from the outside of the profile.

  6. To reverse the extrude direction, click Reverse Direction.
