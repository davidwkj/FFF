Moving Faces

You can copy, move, rotate, and offset faces.

  1. From the section of the action bar, click Move Face .

  2. In the Move Face dialog box, select faces to move in the Faces to move box.

  3. Drag the Robot handles or enter values to relocate the faces.

  4. Optional: Click Offset to offset the selected faces. Drag the offset handle in the work area or enter an offset distance in the dialog box.

  5. Optional: Click Copy to keep a copy of the moved faces in the tree.

  6. Optional: Click Align Robot to align Robot axes with selections.

    Option Description


    		U-Axis. Aligns the Robot u-axis with your selection.


    		V-Axis. Aligns the Robot v-axis with your selection.


    		W-Axis. Aligns the Robot w-axis with your selection.


  7. Click when finished with the Move Face command.
