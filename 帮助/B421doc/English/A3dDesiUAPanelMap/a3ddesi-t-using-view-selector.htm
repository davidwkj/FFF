Using the View Selector

You can use the View Selector to see what the right, left, front, back, top, and isometric views of the product. The View Selector also lets you select additional standard views.

  1. Do one of the following:

    • Press the spacebar.
    • From the View section of the action bar, click View Selector .

  2. Hover over View Selector faces to see a preview of the product orientation.

  3. Click a View Selector face.

    The product rotates to the selected view.
