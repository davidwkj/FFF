Extrude Options

You can specify extrude options.

Reverse Side to Cut. Applies to Extrude Cut. Removes material from the outside of the profile.


Sketch Plane Offset Options. Applies an optional offset for the extrusion.



 At Sketch. Extrudes the sketch profile from the sketch plane.


End to end. Applies no offset to the extrusion.


From Offset. Extrudes the sketch profile an offset distance from the original position of the sketch plane.


From Selected Geometry. Extrudes the sketch profile an offset distance from the selected geometry.

Reverse Direction. Reverses the direction of the extrusion along the selected vector.
Draft 1. Tapers the faces of the feature using a specified angle for Direction 1 .
Draft 2. Tapers the faces of the feature using a specified angle for Direction 2 . This option is available when an end condition is selected for Direction 2.
Along Vector. Extrudes in the direction defined by a selected face, edge, or axis.
Auto Merge.
