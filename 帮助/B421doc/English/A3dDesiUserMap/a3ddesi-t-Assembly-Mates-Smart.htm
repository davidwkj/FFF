Smart Mating

You can select two components in the work area and have the app automatically detect the possible mating points between the two selected components.

  1. From the Assembly section of the action bar, create or insert the components.

  2. Select a face of one component.

  3. Hover over another component.

    A mating ball is displayed at each potential mating location.

  4. Click the mating ball.

    All possible mating solutions appear in the mate context menu.

  5. Click the appropriate mate icon in the context menu.

    The components reposition to commit to the selected mate.
