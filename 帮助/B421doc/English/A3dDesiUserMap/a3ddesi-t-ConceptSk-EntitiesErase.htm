Erasing and Deleting Curves from Freehand Sketches

For freehand curves, you can erase portions of curves or delete entire curves.

  1. From the Freehand section of the action bar, click Erase or Delete .

  2. Optional: For Erase , specify the width of the eraser stroke:

    Option Description
    Eraser Size. Change the width of the eraser stroke by moving the slider or entering a value.

  3. Drag the stylus or pointer across curves to erase portions or delete entire curves.
