Applying a View Style

You can change line and background style of your sketch and work area affecting the appearance; such as color tone, line sharpness, and shading.

  • From the View section of the action bar, click the style to apply to the work area.

    The following styles are available:

    None

    		 Lavis

    		 Sketchy
    Graphite

    		 Water Color
