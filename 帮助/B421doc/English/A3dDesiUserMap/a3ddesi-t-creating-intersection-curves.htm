Creating Intersection Curves

You can create wireframe geometry by intersecting elements. You can intersect sketch entities, solid bodies, and surfaces.

  1. From the Modeling section of the action bar, click Intersection .

  2. In Items to be Intersected , select one or more items from the work area to be intersected.

  3. In Items to Intersect , select one or more items from the work area to intersect.

  4. Optional: Select Extrapolate Intersections to extrapolate the intersection boundaries.
