Inserting Existing Components

You can insert an existing product as a component of the current product.

  1. From the Assembly section of the action bar, click Insert Existing Component .

  2. From the top bar, use Search to locate the component.

    For example, type a name or partial name and press Enter.

  3. From the search results, drag the product to the work area, drop it, and then click to place it.

    The component is inserted in the product and appears in the design tree.

    Note: To insert additional instances of the component, Ctrl + drag the component from the design tree and drop it in the work area.
