Listing External References

In the External References Manager, you can view a list of external references that exist in a product and examine which items (such as faces and edges) are involved in each external reference.

Context:

Note: In the design tree, indicates items that have external references.

  1. From the Assembly section of the action bar, click External References Manager .

  2. In the dialog box, click Update All References to incorporate any recent changes.

  3. In the list, you can do the following:

    • Expand items to see geometry involved in the external reference.
    • Select items to highlight them in the design tree and work area.

  4. Click to close the External References Manager.
