Moving a Body

You can move and rotate a body parametrically.

  1. From the Modeling section of the action bar, click Move Body .

  2. Select one or more bodies to move.

  3. To define the required rotation angle, drag the Robot to a face, vertex, or edge, or select a Robot position option:

    Option Description
