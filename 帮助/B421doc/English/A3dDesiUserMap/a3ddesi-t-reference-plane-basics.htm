Creating a Reference Plane

You can create planes to reference during operations such as sketching, creating a section view, and designating a neutral plane for a draft feature.

  1. From the Modeling section of the action bar, click Reference Plane .

  2. Select the items that define the plane. For example: faces, edges, or sketch entities.

    Depending on your selections, various handles, dimension and selection boxes might appear in the dialog box.

  3. To reposition the reference plane:

    • Drag the arrow handle.
    • Click the dimension field and enter a value.

  4. To resize the preview of the plane, edit the feature and then drag the circle handles.

    Note: If a multisolution option appears, you can click through the offerings to view or select other solutions.
