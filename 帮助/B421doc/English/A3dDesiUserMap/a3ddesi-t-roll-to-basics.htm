Rolling Back in the Design Tree

You can roll features in the design tree back to a selection, suppressing subsequent features in the tree.

When you roll the model back in the design tree, you revert the model to an earlier state. You can add new features or edit existing features in the reverted model. You can also drag the rollback bar forward or backward in the design tree.

  • Right-click a feature in the design tree and click Roll To .

    The features that follow your selection in the design tree are suppressed, and a blue rollback bar appears below your selection in the design tree.



    You can drag the rollback bar up or down to modify your feature selection.
