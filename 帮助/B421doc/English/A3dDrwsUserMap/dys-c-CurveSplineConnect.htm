Connection and Continuity Options

Tools Palette contains connection and continuity options for defining the connection that you need.

Connection Options

Connection options are:

  • Connect with an Arc: .
  • Connect with a Spline: (default option).

Continuity options

Continuity options are (available with Connect with a Spline only):

  • Continuity in point:

  • Continuity in tangency: (default option).

  • Continuity in curvature: (selected by default).

Tension

The tension value corresponds to a multiplying coefficient applied to the tangent vector norm (available with Continuity in tangency and Continuity in curvature options only). The default value is one and the zero value corresponds to a continuity in point.

About the Connect Command

This topic provides information needed about Connect command.

Keep in mind the following when using Connect :

  • You can move the connecting curve: in this case, the shape of the support elements will change accordingly, as shown here for example.

  • You cannot trim nor break connecting curves. To workaround this, you can project the connecting curve of interest onto another sketch, then trim the projected elements.
