Complementing an Arc (Circle or Ellipse)

You can get the complement of an arc (circle or an ellipse) using Complement command.

Before you begin: Create an arc.
Related Topics
Creating Circles
Creating Ellipses

  1. Select the arc to be complemented.



  2. To complement the arc you can either:

    • From the Sketch section of the action bar, click Complement .
    • Right-click the arc and select Circle.n > Complement.

    The complementary arc is created.
