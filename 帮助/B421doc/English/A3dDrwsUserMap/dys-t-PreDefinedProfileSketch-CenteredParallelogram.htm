Creating Parallelograms

  1. Optional:

    Performing this step results into a centered parallelogram with two parallelism constraints as long as two symmetrical constraints which are based on the two selected lines

  2. From the Sketch section of the action bar, click Centered Parallelogram .

  3. Select a line (or an axis).

  4. Select another line (or an axis).

  5. Move the pointer to specify the dimensions.

    The parallelogram is created. It is centered on the intersection point of the two lines. Its edges are parallel to the selected lines.
