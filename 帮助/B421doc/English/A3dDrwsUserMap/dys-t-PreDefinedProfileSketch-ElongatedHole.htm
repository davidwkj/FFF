Creating Elongated Holes

You can create an elongated hole.

Before you begin:
Related Topics
Creating Cylindrical Elongated Holes

  1. From the Sketch section of the action bar, click Elongated Hole .

    Tools Palette now displays boxes for defining the elongated hole center to center axis (first and second center point) and then either the elongated hole radius or a point on this elongated hole.

  2. Enter the coordinates for the first center point of the elongated hole in Tools Palette and press Enter.

  3. Enter the coordinates for the second center point and press Enter.

    The profile's major axis is created using these points.

    Note: You can also specify the length and angle of this axis.

  4. Enter the coordinates for a point on the profile and press Enter.

    The profile's minor axis is defined. In other words, the elongated hole's width is defined applying a given radius to the profile's extremities.

    Note: You can also specify the radius of the elongated hole.

The elongated hole is created.



Note: you can create this elongated hole manually by activating SmartPick and clicking as you get the required option.
