Creating Keyhole Profiles

You can sketch the profile for keyhole using the predefined profile.

Before you begin:

  1. From the Sketch section of the action bar, click Keyhole Profile .

    Tools Palette displays boxes for defining the keyhole profile.

  2. Enter the coordinates for the stating point in Tools Palette and press Enter.

  3. Enter the coordinates for the small radius center point and press Enter.

  4. Click to define the small radius.

  5. Click to define the large radius.

    The keyhole is created.

    Note: you can create this keyhole profile manually by activating SmartPick and clicking as you get the required option.
