Creating Hyperbolas by Focus

You can create a hyperbola.

Before you begin: Make sure that the Open dialog boxes with legacy appearance check box available at Me > Preferences > All Preferenes > 3D Modelling > 3D Modeling Core > Sketcher > Dialogs appearance, is selected.
Related Topics
Creating Parabolas by Focus

  1. From the Sketch section of the action bar, click Hyperbola by Focus .

  2. Click the location of the focus in work area to define the focus point.

    The focus is symbolized by a cross ().

  3. Click in work area to define center (asymptote intersection) point.

    The center is not associative to the hyperbola.

  4. Click in work area to define apex point.



  5. Click two points that correspond to the hyperbola's end points.

    1. Click a point to define one end point of the hyperbola.



    2. Click another point to define other end point of the hyperbola.

Tip: You can use Tools Palette for defining the eccentricity of the hyperbola.
