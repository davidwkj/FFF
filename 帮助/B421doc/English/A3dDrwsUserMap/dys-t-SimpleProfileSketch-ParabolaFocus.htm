Creating Parabolas by Focus

You can create a parabola.

Before you begin: Make sure that the Open dialog boxes with legacy appearance check box available at Me > Preferences > All Preferenes > 3D Modelling > 3D Modeling Core > Sketcher > Dialogs appearance, is selected.
Related Topics
Creating Hyperbolas by Focus

  1. From the Sketch section of the action bar, click Parabola by Focus .

  2. Click the location of the focus in work area to define the focus point.



  3. Click in work area to define a apex point.



  4. Click two points that correspond to the parabola's end points:

    1. Click a point to define one end point of the parabola.



    2. Click another point to define other end point of the parabola.
