Creating a Point

You can create a point.

Before you begin:
  • Ensure that the is selected from the Sketch section of the action bar.
  • Make sure that the Open dialog boxes with legacy appearance check box available at Me > Preferences > All Preferenes > 3D Modelling > 3D Modeling Core > Sketcher > Dialogs appearance, is selected.

About Points

  1. From the Sketch section of the action bar, click Point by Clicking .

  2. Locate the point.

    You can:

    • Click anywhere in the work area.
    • Type the point coordinates in Tools Palette: H=24 mm, V=22 mm and press Enter.

    The point is created.

Tip: The constraints are assigned to this point as per the previously selected in the Sketch section of the action bar.
