Creating Three Point Circles

You can create a circle passing through three known points.

Before you begin:

About Creating Circles

  1. From the Sketch section of the action bar, click Three Point Circle .

  2. In the H and V boxes of the Tools Palette enter the coordinates of the three points one after another.

    You can also locate the points by clicking the point's location in the work area.

    Note: Depending upon the point selection, the radius is calculated and displayed in the R box in the Tools Palette

    The three point circle is created.
