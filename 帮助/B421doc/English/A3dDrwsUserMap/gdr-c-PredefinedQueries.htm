About Search

You can search for content from the top bar or from within apps, such as when filling in a field in a form or adding an item to a list. The app uses 3DSearch to find content. When searching, you can use the 6WTags to refine your searches.

For more information about using 3DSearch, including how to filter with 6WTags and use predefined queries, see 3DEXPERIENCE Basics: Searching for Content.
