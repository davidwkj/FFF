Flyout for Creating Predefined Profiles

Rectangle
Creates a rectangle.
Oriented Rectangle
Creates a rectangle at an orientation to the sketch plane using three known vertices.
Parallelogram
Creates a parallelogram when three of the vertices are known.
Polygon
Creates a polygon with number of sides ranging from three to 24.
Centered Rectangle
Creates a rectangle when the center and one of the corners are known.
Centered Parallelogram
Creates a parallelogram when the center and one of the corners are known.

Flyout for Creating Circles

Circle from Point
Creates a circle using the selected point as center.
Three Point Circle
Creates a circle when three points lying on the circle are known.
Circle Using Coordinates
Creates a circle with Cartesian or polar coordinates.
Tri-Tangent Circle
Creates a circle tangent to three elements.
Three Point Arc
Creates an arc with three points.
Three Point Arc Starting With Limits
Creates an arc with three points starting with the end points first.
Arc
Creates an arc with the center point and the end points.
Ellipse
Creates an ellipse when the center, the major radius, and the minor radius are known.

Flyout for Creating Lines

Line
Creates a line with two end points.
Infinite Line
Creates an endless line.
Bi-Tangent Line
Creates a line tangent to two elements.
Bisecting Line
Creates an angle bisector for two given lines.
Line Normal To Curve
Creates a line normal to the curve.

Flyout for Creating Points

Point
Create a point.
Point Using Coordinates
Creates a point with Cartesian or polar coordinates.
Equidistant Points
Creates a set of equidistant points.
Intersection Point
Creates points of intersection of two elements.
Projection Point
Creates the projection of a point on an element.
Align Points
Aligns the selected points onto a single line.
