Copying and Pasting Elements

You can copy and paste drawing elements.

Before you begin: Open a drawing representation.
Related Topics
About Copy/Paste

  1. Select the element you want to cut or copy.

  2. Use any one of the following ways to copy an item:

    • From the standard area of the action bar, click Copy .
    • Press Ctrl+C.
    • Right-click the element and select Copy .

    The copied item is placed in the clipboard.

  3. Use any one of the following ways to paste the copied item:

    • Click Paste .
    • Press Ctrl+V.
    • Right-click the view frame and select Paste.
