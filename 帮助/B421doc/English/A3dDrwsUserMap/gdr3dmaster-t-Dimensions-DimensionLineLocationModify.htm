Modifying the Dimension Line Location

You can modify the dimension line location either as you create or after creating dimensions.

Related Topics
Creating Dimensions

  1. Double-click the dimension to be modified.

    The dimension is highlighted.



  2. Select the dimension line or the extension line.

  3. Drag it to the new position and click to validate.



    Tip: You can press Shift to temporarily activate/deactivate snapping (depending on whether the Snap value between symbols (SHIFT toggles) check box is selected in Me > Preferences > All Preferences > 3D Modeling > Mechanical Systems > Drafting > Dimension section).
