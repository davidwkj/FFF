Creating Axis Lines and Center Lines

You can create axis and center lines on several circles simultaneously.

Related Topics
Modifying Center Lines or Axis Lines

  1. From the Annotation section of the action bar, click Axis Line and Center Line .

  2. Select the circles on which you want to create the axis lines and center lines.



    The axes and center lines are created.



  3. Select an axis line handle and drag it along a direction. You can notice that thread axis lines are modified symmetrically.

Tip: If you want to move only one axis line, hold on Ctrl while you are dragging the handle.

Note: When creating axes and center lines in a Drawings context, a message is displayed if axes and center line cannot be associative to the 3D.
