Modifying Center Lines or Axis Lines

You can modify center lines or axis lines at one or more of their ends.

Before you begin: Open a drawing representation.
Related Topics
Creating Axis Lines and Center Lines

  1. Select one or several center lines or axis lines.

    End points appear.

  2. Select any end point and drag to move all the line extremities to a new position.



  3. Press Ctrl while selecting any end point and drag the selected extremity to a new position.



    Tip: You can also modify center lines or axis lines through Edit > Properties > Graphic tab.
