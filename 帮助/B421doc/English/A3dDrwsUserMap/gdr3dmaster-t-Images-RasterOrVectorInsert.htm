Inserting Images (Raster or Vector)

You can insert a picture in a representation.

Before you begin: Open a representation containing a sheet and a view.
Related Topics
About Inserting Images
Picture Properties
Editing Raster Images

  1. From the Annotation section of the action bar, click Insert Picture .

  2. In the Insert picture dialog box, browse to select an image file.

  3. Click Open.

  4. Click in the sheet to position your picture.

  5. Click the picture to select it.

    Rotation and scaling handles appear.

  6. Optional: Drag the handles to modify the picture size, and/or drag the picture to the required position.
