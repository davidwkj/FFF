Browsing and Editing Templates: Template Browser

You can navigate between the different templates in your sheet with the panel of the view browser and organize them.

Templates is a panel representation in your sheet which shows all the templates with details or as thumbnails. The number next to the title of the panel signifies the number of templates in the active detail sheet.

Before you begin:

From the View section of the action bar, click Template Browser . The Templates panel appears in the work area. Click again to hide it. By default, the panel is activated.

  1. In the Templates panel, you can display the templates in your sheet in different formats:

    1. Click to display in thumbnails view.
    2. Click to display in details view.

    The thumbnail view displays the following:

    • The icon of the type of template
    • The name

    The list view displays the following:

    • The icon of the type of template
    • The name
    • The scale
    • The keywords generated at catalog creation, if any

  2. Hover over a thumbnail to view the scale in a tooltip.

  3. Hover over a list item in the details view to view the scale and keywords (if any).

  4. Right-click the thumbnail or the list item to access other commands.
