Creating a Freeform Disk

You can create a component based on the shape of a disk, then customize that shape into a unique design.

  1. From the Freeform section of the action bar, click Disk .

    A disk is displayed in the work area.

  2. Select a plane or planar face for the freeform.

    Note: Repeatedly press q to cycle through the orientation of the freeform on the various planes.

  3. Click in the work area to place the disk in the approximate location you want.

  4. Click in the text boxes and enter the number of subdivision in the mesh.



  5. Drag the arrow to define the size.

  6. Click in Freeform Disk dialog box.

  7. Optional: Drag the Robot arrows to position the disk.

  8. Optional: Drag the mesh points and faces to customize the shape of the disk.

    To assist with selecting the mesh entities, the following options are available.

    • Press o to select the faces around the freeform. Click near an edge of the face, to select the direction of the loop.
    • Press i to select the ring around the freeform.
